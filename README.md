# BMW Sales Data Analysis (SQL Project)

## 📊 Overview

This project analyzes BMW vehicle sales data from **2010–2024** with a focus on the **African market**.
The objective was to investigate declining sales and determine whether pricing strategies influenced customer demand.

Using SQL, the project transforms raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support pricing and market decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions

* Which vehicle models generate the most revenue in Africa?
* Have price increases contributed to declining sales volume?
* Are customers price-sensitive or driven by brand prestige?
* What strategies can improve regional performance?

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies

* **MySQL Workbench**
* SQL (Joins, Aggregations, Calculated Fields)
* Window Functions (`LAG`) for year-over-year comparison
* Stored Procedures for reusable analysis

---

## 📈 Key Analysis Performed

### 1. Revenue Trend Analysis

Calculated total revenue (`price × sales_volume`) to identify:

* Top-performing models
* Revenue growth and decline over time

### 2. Price Elasticity Analysis

Used window functions to compare yearly price and volume changes to determine:

* Customer sensitivity to price adjustments
* Whether demand responds to discounts or prestige pricing

### 3. Model Performance Investigation

Segmented data by model, fuel type, and transmission to evaluate which configurations drive regional sales.

---

## 💡 Key Insights

* Sales volume declined as prices increased, indicating **price sensitivity** in the market.
* Certain premium models maintained demand despite price increases, showing **brand-driven purchasing behavior**.
* Pricing stability and accessible financing may improve long-term growth.
